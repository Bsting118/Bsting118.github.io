Personal Info

Name: Brendan Sting

Age: 21 years

Gender: Male (He/Him)

By now, you already know my name, but what you probably don't know is that I love bowling, skeet shooting, making video games, and even building PC's with my dad! Heck, I even have a 1983 Compaq Portable computer that I am still repairing; trust me when I say that removing old arrays of RAM chips is hard work!

To give a rundown on my path in life, I grew up in the humble state of Michigan where generally I would spend my days bike-riding, bowling, and playing games with my family. Later on in high school, I took an intro to computer science course with Python and Alice which made me instantly fall in love with programming. Coding granted me the ablility to create digital landscapes and handle computer data to make whatever my imagination desired... which usually ended up being video games. After high school, I went to Kettering University to try and amp up my career, both as a software developer and as a game developer, using their famous co-op program. I got a little spooked when a lot of my peers dropped out of the gaming concentration that I was in due to its intensity, but I had faith that my hardwork, dedication, and passion would get me through.

Currently, I'm in my senior year at Kettering, still holding that CS gaming concentration title with pride! Additionally, I've had the pleasure of working at Nexteer Automotive where I studied multidisciplinary software development in manufacturing and production environments, which now I utilize to lead the development of enterprise software tools for Nexteer's EPS Core Mechanical CAE team!
